About Me

Hey. I'm Luminalia -- Luma or Lumi for short, and if we're actually close, just Lumi. My name came from a Battle Cats unit I love: Lumina, the Legend Rare Cat. She/her or they/them both work.

18, born and raised in Ontario, Canada on May 2nd, 2008. I've been in the furry community for seven years. My fursona is a raccoon named Ajax -- his story is still being written.

I'm a deathcore vocalist for local bands, a writer, and someone with strong opinions I'll probably share without being asked. Fair warning.

The way I communicate depends on the situation. Left to my own devices I speak in ways people describe as complex or cryptic -- I think in systems and explain things in layers. Around most people I tend to over-explain and slow things down out of habit. Years of guiding people who needed more context just wired me that way. I don't think less of anyone for it. It's just something I'm working on.

I lead with logic. I'll argue my positions seriously and update them when someone gives me a genuine reason to. What I can't work with is someone who won't engage at all.

How I Love

I'm a hopeless romantic in the most genuine sense. The relationship I'm looking for is one of the primary reasons I keep going. I feel love harder and more intensely than most people around me.

My love languages are physical affection, verbal affection (praise especially), and gift giving. I have trouble accepting gifts -- I feel like I haven't earned them -- but they hit very deep when they happen. No surprises though -- that causes me real anxiety.

Extremely monogamous. Opposed to polyamory. Not changing. I also have a housewife mentality -- taking care of someone is genuinely how I feel useful and loved at the same time.

I have bad jealousy. If you're in the same room and not paying attention to me, or talking to someone else at length, I get sad and moody. I know this. I'm working on it.

I have serious attachment issues -- once attached, it's all-consuming. Things outside the relationship tend to slip. Working on it with professionals.

I have strong cuteness aggression. If you do something cute I get an overwhelming urge to affectionately attack you. Genuine, not harmful. Just how I'm wired.

Love and sex are intertwined for me. I need both -- one without the other doesn't function.
